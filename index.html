EASTSIDE
ESPORT

TOGETHER WE WIN
TOGETHER WE LEARN

Eastside Esport is a community of passionate esports enthusiasts who love to play, watch, and learn about all things gaming.

Our club is made up of 16-25 year old boys who share a common interest in esports and gaming culture.

We are fortunate to have a team of experienced coaches who are dedicated to helping our members improve their gameplay and achieve their goals. Our coaches are passionate about esports and bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our community.

We believe that esports is more than just a hobby; it's a way of life. Our club provides a space for gamers to connect, compete, and learn from each other.

Everyone should have the opportunity to participate in esports, regardless of their skill level or experience.

OUR VALUES

FUN

We believe that gaming should be fun, and we enjoy playing together as a team. We don't take ourselves too seriously and enjoy the experience of playing with others.

TEAMWORK

We understand that working together as a team is essential for success. We support each other, communicate effectively, and work towards our common goals.

RESPECT

We value respect and treat everyone with kindness and dignity. We believe that everyone deserves to be treated fairly, whether they are our teammates, opponents, or spectators.
